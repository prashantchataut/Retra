# Design Taste — Retra Adaptation

Start with a one-line design read and three dials (variance, motion, density). Avoid generic AI-purple marketing layouts, repetitive equal cards, excessive centered composition, decorative motion, and visually loud surfaces without hierarchy. For Retra: premium retro-gaming OS; variance 7, motion 5, density 6. Preserve product usability over web-landing-page theatrics.
