# Retra Product Principles

## Product Position

Retra is a personal memory player for handheld games, not a technical ROM manager. It should feel emotionally warm, fast, private, and reliable.

## Design Principles

1. **Return, do not administer.** The primary action is continuing a game, not browsing settings or file paths.
2. **Artwork carries emotion.** Use cover art and game identity as the visual foreground; chrome should recede.
3. **Nostalgia without imitation.** Avoid Poké Ball geometry, Nintendo hardware silhouettes, copyrighted characters, or console trade dress in Retra branding.
4. **Glass with discipline.** Reserve translucent layers for navigation, hero actions, overlays, and grouping. Keep text surfaces readable.
5. **Local is a feature.** Clearly communicate that games and saves remain on-device and that accounts are optional.
6. **One tap means trustworthy.** A downloadable catalog item is one-tap only after source, license, HTTPS, supported type, size, and checksum validation.
7. **Quiet confidence.** Avoid gamer clichés, crowded stat dashboards, fake social proof, and novelty motion.

## Visual Personality

- Dark mode is cinematic, not black-on-neon.
- Light mode is soft lavender-white, not sterile gray.
- Headings are bold and compact; supporting text is calm and plain.
- Rounded geometry is generous but not toy-like.
- Motion should confirm hierarchy and continuity, never delay play.
